## Component Selection Criteria
- **Price**: Budget constraints vs performance needs.
- **Compatibility**: Ensure components work well together (check datasheets).
- **Availability**: Local vs online availability.
- **Reviews**: User reviews & ratings for reliability.

## Power Supply Requirements
- Ensure power supply is capable of providing at least 500mA at 5V for the setup.

## Tools Needed for Assembly
- Soldering iron, wire cutters, pliers, multimeter, screwdriver.

## Component Compatibility Notes
- The ESP32-CAM can be powered directly from the WEMOS D1 R1 for streamlined wiring. Ensure power ratings match.

## Alternatives for Each Component
- **ESP32-CAM**: ESP8266 based camera modules.
- **WEMOS D1 R1**: NodeMCU v2, Raspberry Pi PICO.
- **DHT11**: DHT22 (more accurate).
- **HC-SR04**: SRF05 (wider measurement range).

## Quality Checking Tips
- Check solder joints for cold solder or shorts, test components individually before assembly.

## Storage and Handling Recommendations
- Store components in anti-static bags, avoid humidity, keep away from direct sunlight.
